Thanks For Showing Up For The Arts!

Our Work Is Not Done

Contact Your City Council Member Today

Contact your city councilmember to urge them to support full re-instatement of arts and culture funding! 


Councilmembers Sean Elo-Rivera, Henry Foster, Vivian Moreno, and Kent Lee have proposed an alternate budget plan that proposes some restoration of arts and culture funding. However, we need full restoration of arts and culture funding to ensure organizations of all sizes throughout the City can survive.


Please write to your San Diego City Councilmember by doing the following:

  • Find your City Council District using this map, then find your Councilmember’s contact information here.

  • Draft your own letter or visit here to complete an easy webform letter that will be submitted to the Mayor and City Councilmembers, along with information on the importance of the arts.

We need everyone to speak up and urge City councilmembers to protect the arts organizations you care about.


Hundreds Showed Up to Support the Arts!

Hundreds Showed Up to Support the Arts!

Hundreds of San Diegans showed up for the Arts on Wednesday, May 27!  A rally and press conference were held on the Civic Center Plaza prior to the City Council Budget meeting. During the meeting, over 200 San Diegans signed up to provide comment on the Mayor's revised budget proposal. The meeting lasted until just before midnight when the last comments were heard.



David Bennett, Joann Clark General Director and CEO, recently spoke at the San Diego City Council budget meeting.

Thank you for ensuring the arts are supported in San Diego!
